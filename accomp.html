SHAREON By Teknesya Networks Private Limited


As we are talking about using Indian Apps, this is a very small sized wonderful file transfer interface developed by Jyotirmoy Saha and team, University of Engineering & Management (UEM), Jaipur, through his very successful startup Teknesya Networks. As he writes:- " ShareOn, just an application of 3.2 MB, installing it will really help you in promoting the idea of #makeinindia. ShareOn presents the most user-friendly UI for file transfer applications available to date and it transfers files in the blink of eyes. ShareOn, despite being such a small-sized application is not less than much in comparison to other file transfer applications like SHAREit and Xender. Come on guys don't be selfish Besides thinking of installing it you should also share this message with others and make a step forward in #makeinindia move. Believe in Indian products and you can once more make India independent.
